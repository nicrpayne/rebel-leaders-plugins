# Sword Glow Color Analysis

From the reference images, the Truth Sword emits a bright cyan/ice-blue glow.
The color appears to be approximately:
- Core blade: bright white-cyan (#B0F0FF to #FFFFFF)
- Inner glow: cyan-blue (#00D4FF / #4DE8FF)  
- Outer glow/ambient: softer cyan (#00A8CC at ~20-30% opacity)
- The glow casts onto the surrounding dark wood and stone pedestal
